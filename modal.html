About Ikigai and the Game

Ikigai is a Japanese concept that means "a reason for being". It is the intersection of

  • what you love,
  • what you are good at,
  • what the world needs, and
  • what you can be paid for.

Most of us are missing one of these circles. In this game, you will embark on a journey to unlock your complete Ikigai by solving a variety of puzzles associated with these four aspects.
